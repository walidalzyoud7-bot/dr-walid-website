Introduction to the GJU Genomics Lab

Founded in 2023 by Principal Investigator (PI) Walid Al-Zyoud, the GJU Genomics Lab at the German Jordanian University is a dedicated research facility specializing in high-throughput genomic technologies.

The lab’s core platforms include Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), real-time PCR (qPCR), and associated molecular biology tools. Research focuses on infectious diseases, molecular pathology, and biomarker discovery through translational genomics approaches.

The lab also oversees biosafety compliance, protocol development, equipment acquisition, and the training of graduate and undergraduate researchers, collaborating with both local and international partners.
