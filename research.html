Research Interests

Molecular Pathology, Biotechnology & Biomedical Applications

My research focuses on molecular pathology, biotechnology, and biomedical applications with a focus on developing laboratory, computational, and bioimaging methods for disease identification.

A one-year clinical research fellowship at UC San Diego provided formal training in drug clinical trial methods, research ethics, and epidemiological study design, strengthening an ongoing commitment to global and translational research in infectious, respiratory, and viral diseases (e.g., COVID-19).
